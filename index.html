Most Relevant Model for ASA

Selldorf Architects: the website IS the brand statement.

A boutique New York firm. Restrained, elegant, precise. Black-and-white palette lets colorful photography dominate entirely.

Its differentiator is on the homepage: "Since its inception the firm's design ethos has been deeply rooted in the principles of humanism."

The lesson:A small firm's website can convey quiet authority without flash. You don't need scale to compete.

This is the model for ASA's redesign.

Chapter Eight

Phase 3:
Full Redesign

Months 4–8 · Significant Investment

Migrate. Redesign. Differentiate.

Platform: Wix → Webflow

  • Code-free design flexibility
  • Built-in CMS for projects & blog
  • Excellent SEO tools
  • Fast hosting on AWS
  • Client-editable without developer

Visual Direction: Selldorf Model

Restrained palette of black, white & warm greys. Monochromatic interface lets project photography dominate.

One refined sans-serif family. Generous, intentional whitespace — framing photography like a gallery wall.
